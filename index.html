About Dalph

We believe technology is a powerful force for good and we are working to foster a sustainable and level playing platform where everyone has access to and can be empowered by the benefits and opportunities created by technology. We are leveraging on technology to provide a number one go to market platform where talents, skills, services and expertise meets demand thereby creating economic opportunities and lifestyle

Our Vision

To be the number one market place where everyone can buy and sell skills, services and expertise without breaking a sweat.

Our Mission

To empower everyone to work their passion and bring economic prosperity and lifestyle to everyone in everywhere.

What We Do

At Dalph, we help every person and every organization to get more done by providing reliable access to a large pool of seasoned talents, skills, services and professionalism and, giving those offering one service or the other the freedom to look for and find jobs online with ease. This we do by connecting proven talents, skills, services and expertise to those demanding it.

Our Core Values

Our values shape our organization's culture and define the character of our company. We live the core values through individual behaviour and they serve as the basis for how we act and make decisions.

Client Obsession
Our clients are our most prized asset. We are determined to create meaningful relationships with our clients by being responsive, staying relevant and by continously delivering value to our clients' experience.
Respect
We understand that the opinions, feelings, and circumstances of others are as important as ours and this we do by valuing diversity and unique contributions, finding common grounds, nurturing a trusting, open and inclusive environment and treating each person in a manner that reflects Dalph's values.
Integrity
We conduct ourselve honestly in all things, and we let our actions speak for who we are and what we believe in. We are uncompromising in our ideals and we inspire trust by being consistent with our core values, matching our behaviors to our words and accepting responsibility for our actions.
Accountabilty and Stewardship
We believe accountability starts with us and we accept full responsibility for our decisions, actions, and results. We also feel strongly obligated to fulfill our company's vision of building a one stop market place where every person can buy and sell proven talents, skills and expertise without breaking a sweat, protecting our customers' interests and experiences and meeting our commitment to bringing economic prosperity and lifestyle to everyone.
